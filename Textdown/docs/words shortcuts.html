Words Shortcuts

Words shortcuts are user-configured words to Textdown expand. You can use then to shortcut your email, glyphs, emojis.

There are two ways to expand a word shortcut:

  • Press space, type the shortcut, then press esc and Textdown will replace it.
  • Select the shortcut, then press esc and Textdown will replace it. Also a little tip is to use shift to make a selection without using the mouse: hold down shift and make the selection using the the arrows keys.
